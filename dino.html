The dinosaurs that we know of and imagine may be completely wrong. New technology has allowed us to discover that some dinosaurs actually had feathers.
For example, the Velociraptor were found to have "quill knobs" on the forearm bones. These knobs are where wing feathers anchor, and provide conclusive evidence for winged, bird-like Velociraptors.
