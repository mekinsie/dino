The dinosaurs that we know of and imagine may be completely wrong. New technology has allowed us to discover that some dinosaurs actually had feathers. According to BBC, the Velociraptor was found to have quill knobs on the forearm bones of a fossil. These knobs are where wing feathers anchor, and provide conclusive evidence for winged, bird-like Velociraptors.
